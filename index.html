About

I have extensive experience in the biological sciences. That is where my coding journey began. I needed a way to analyze viral genomic sequences that was cheap. I started to learn to code in order to make my own scripts that would analyze my data. I then wanted to design an interface where others could use my scripts for their own data. This lead me on the path of web design and development.


I have grown to love developing web sites for various purposes. It is a great way for me to express my creative side.


Please contact me and we can discuss how I can help design your web site. Whether it's a personal website or a site for your business, I am confident that you will be satisfied with the outcome.
